Journey

Timeline of SDAC milestones.

The path from a small mentorship idea to a movement shaping futures.

  1. 2018

    Concept sparks

    Founders begin meeting with youth leaders to design a mentorship model rooted in community voice.

  2. 2019

    Pilot cohort

    First 25 students complete the SDAC pilot, launching the community feed and book club.

  3. 2021

    Scaling citywide

    Partnerships with three schools bring mobile mentorship labs to hundreds of students.

  4. 2023

    Launch of SDAC Studios

    We open a dedicated space for content creation, prototyping, and youth-led showcases.

  5. 2024

    Scholarship fund

    Community donors and partners seed scholarships for alumni launching ventures and heading to college.
