Pathway from interest to leadership

We meet youth where they are, then layer in mindset coaching, skill labs, and public showcases to grow confidence step by step.

  • Onboarding huddles identify each member’s goals and strengths.
  • Mentors co-design learning playlists with resources from our library.
  • Monthly showcases celebrate progress and connect families and partners.
Inside SDAC

What a 90-day journey looks like

Each cohort cycles through focus weeks so members can explore, build, and lead:

  • Weeks 1-4 — Foundations & vision setting with mentor check-ins.
  • Weeks 5-8 — Skill labs and field trips with partner organizations.
  • Weeks 9-12 — Demo day, reflection, and planning the next milestone.
