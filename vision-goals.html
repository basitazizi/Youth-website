North star

Vision & goals for the next decade.

SDAC imagines a world where every young person has a mentor, a pathway, and a community cheering them on.

Vision

Mentorship as a right

Every student deserves consistent coaching, meaningful projects, and access to networks that open doors.

Mission

Equip & amplify youth voice

We provide tools, storytelling platforms, and funding that allow young people to lead solutions.

Promise

Community first

SDAC programs are co-created with families, educators, and alumni, ensuring cultural relevance and sustainability.

Goals we are tracking

Access

  • Mentor 1,000 youth annually across all hubs.
  • Provide every participant with a technology stipend.
  • Launch bilingual programs in three languages.

Impact

  • 90% of fellows enroll in higher education or careers they choose.
  • Scholarship fund supporting 200 alumni per year.
  • Publish annual impact reports led by youth researchers.

Sustainability

  • Train 150 community mentors through SDAC Academy.
  • Build a coalition of 50 employer partners.
  • Establish an endowment to secure core programs.
